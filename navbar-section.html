ℹ️ How the Rate Calculation Works

1. Fetch XAU/USD (gold per troy oz) from api.gold-api.com
2. Fetch USD/INR rate from api.exchangerate-api.com
3. Convert: 24K/g = (XAU_USD × USD_INR) ÷ 31.1035
4. 22K/g = 24K × (22/24)
5. 18K/g = 24K × (18/24)
6. We Pay (Old Gold) = 22K − ₹35 margin
